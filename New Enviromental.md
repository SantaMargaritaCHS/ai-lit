Segment 3: Why So Thirsty? (The "Why")

Video Name: How AI uses our drinking water - Part 2.mp4 (BBC)

URL: gs://ai-literacy-platform-175d4.firebasorage.app/Videos/Student Videos/AI and the Environment/How AI uses our drinking water - Part 2.mp4

Activity Description: The student watches Video Segment 2 (BBC Part 2, 0:00-1:54). This video is purely technical and answers why AI needs water. It explains that AI chips get hot , requiring liquid cooling systems. These systems use clean drinking water in cooling towers, where up to 80% of the water evaporates and is "gone" from the local supply.




Segment 4: The Big Picture (The Scale)

Video Name: AI_s_Hidden_Water_Bill.mp4 (Animated)

URL: gs://ai-literacy-platform-175d4.firebasorage.app/Videos/Student Videos/AI and the Environment/AI_s_Hidden_Water_Bill.mp4

Activity Description: The student watches Video Segment 3 (Animated video, 2:34-3:33). This segment scales up from the single user in Segment 1 to the two massive hidden costs:


Training Cost: The one-time cost to train a model like GPT-3 took 700,000 liters of water, or "enough to fill 280 backyard swimming pools".


Community Cost: It shows the real-world impact on towns like The Dalles, Oregon, where data centers can use nearly a quarter of the town's entire water supply.
